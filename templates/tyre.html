TYRE FIXING

✦ Okaaaay bestie let’s fix that tyre like the independent queen you are

Flat Tyre? Don’t Panic Babe.

  1. Step 1: Park Like a Pro. Pull over somewhere safe & flat. Hazard lights ON. Deep breath. You got this.
  2. Step 2: Grab Your Tools. From the boot, take out:
    • Spare tyre
    • Jack
    • Lug wrench
    (Every car has a secret emergency kit. We love prepared queens.)
  3. Step 3: Loosen the Nuts. Before lifting the car, slightly loosen the wheel nuts (turn left). Not fully — just a little drama, not a breakup.
  4. Step 4: Lift Her Up. Place the jack at the correct spot (check car manual). Raise the car till the tyre is off the ground.
  5. Step 5: Swap That Tyre. Remove nuts fully → take off flat tyre → put the spare on → screw nuts back on.
  6. Step 6: Tighten Like You Mean It. Lower the car. Tighten nuts in a star pattern (criss-cross). Secure. Stable. Stunning.

✦ Safety Babe Reminder

  • If road feels unsafe → call roadside help.
  • Spare tyre = temporary glow-up. Visit a tyre shop soon.
