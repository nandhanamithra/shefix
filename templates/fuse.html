FUSE FIXING

✦ Yesss electrician queen era activated. Fuse Said “Nope”? Let’s Fix It.

Step 1: Power OFF First

Before touching anything — switch off the main power. We do NOT play with electricity, babe.

Step 2: Find the Fuse Box

Usually near the entrance, kitchen, or garage. Open it gently like you’re opening secrets.

Step 3: Spot the Drama Queen

Look for: A switch that flipped down OR a fuse that looks burnt/dark. That’s the troublemaker.

Step 4: If It’s a Circuit Breaker

Just flip it fully OFF → then ON again. Simple reset moment.

Step 5: If It’s a Fuse Wire (Old Type)

Remove the damaged fuse carefully. Replace with same rating fuse (very important). Close it properly. Never guess the rating. Match it exactly.

✦ Safety Babe Reminder

  • If sparks, burning smell, or repeated tripping → CALL AN ELECTRICIAN.
  • Never touch with wet hands.
  • If you feel unsure → it’s okay to get help.
